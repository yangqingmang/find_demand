Hurricane Safety Tips

Preparing for a hurricane requires advance planning and quick action when warnings are issued. Follow these essential safety guidelines to protect yourself and your family.

Before the Hurricane:

  • Create an emergency kit with supplies for at least 3 days
  • Develop a family communication plan
  • Know your evacuation zone and routes
  • Secure your home and property
  • Stay informed through official weather channels

How Hurricane Tracking Works

Modern hurricane tracking combines satellite imagery, weather stations, aircraft reconnaissance, and computer models to provide accurate forecasts and real-time monitoring.

Data Sources:

  • Satellite weather imagery
  • Ocean buoy measurements
  • Aircraft reconnaissance flights
  • Ground-based weather stations
  • Advanced computer modeling systems
